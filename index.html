Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a service appointment? +
You can schedule an appointment by calling us directly, using our online booking system, or visiting our service center during business hours.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept Visa, MasterCard, EcoCash, InnBucks, One Money, and PayNow. All payments are securely processed and directed to our payment number: 0786497932.
Do you offer warranties on your services? +
Yes, we offer warranties on both parts and labor. The specific warranty period depends on the service performed and will be detailed in your service agreement.
How long does a typical engine overhaul take? +
Engine overhauls typically take 3-5 business days, depending on the extent of work needed and parts availability. We'll provide a more accurate timeline after initial diagnostics.
Can I get a cost estimate before authorizing work? +
Absolutely. We provide detailed cost estimates before beginning any work, and we'll contact you for approval if additional repairs are needed beyond the initial estimate.
